Member in Good Standing

Badges of Member in Good Standing are granted to those Ushi members who share the same values with us, and actively help to grow Ushi by inviting their business contacts to join the network.

Ushi asks all Ushi members to invite two business contacts to join the network.

If every Ushi member agrees with this spirit, then Ushi will become an increasingly valuable service for all members. Members who support this spirit will earn the Member in Good Standing badge, to be proudly displayed on your profile.

To recognize this valuable contribution to the community, Members in Good Standing enjoy seven very valuable benefits:

  Regular Free Member Member in Good standing
See "who has visited my profile" No Yes
See detailed profiles of Ushi members beyond 3 degrees No Yes
Join groups Up to 20 groups Up to 40 groups
Search and apply for jobs with annual salary above RMB200,000 No free quota available (except promotion period) 5 job applications monthly, for free
Number of results per search 150 300
Offline events No special benefits Special invitations to our offline events

Best of all, by inviting more contacts to join Ushi, you are helping yourself to maximize your business connections by building your network!
