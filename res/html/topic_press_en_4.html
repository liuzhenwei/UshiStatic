Bill Liao's resume

Bill Liao

Co-founder, Xing.com, Founder, WeForest.com

Founding Member, Ushi

Bill Liao, business coach, speaker, author, entrepreneur, philanthropist and diplomat, is a co-founder of the social network service XING and CEO of Finaxis AG, a privately held company in the financial services industry. Bill is also founder and co-coordinator of Neo.org, a global citizenship initiative, and WeForest.com, which will reforest twenty million square kilometers of stripped land.

Bill is an experienced business coach currently mentoring many start-up companies to success and also a speaker where he has shared his wisdom with many international and national audiences including The Dublin Web Summit, The London School of Economics, The World Economic Forum Meeting of The New Champions in China and so on.
